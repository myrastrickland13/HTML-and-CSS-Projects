Mist by Wei Feng Ji Xu

Mist is a web novel English Translation of a Chinese novel. If you are into time travel with a mix of sci-fi, mystery, and thriller, this book is perfect for you. It kept me so enthralled, I finished the 103 chapters, plus 3 extras, in two days. It focuses on the character Ji Yushi, a person with hyperthymesia.

Those with this condition can remember every detail of their lives from something as significant as the worldâ€™s turning point to something as small and as insignificant as a minor fleeting thought. They cannot forget and their thirst for knowledge allows them to be considered a genius in some senses.
Along with the other main character Song Qinglan and their squad, they will traverse different worlds in different times. This includes zombies, another multiverse, and more crazy things like a deadly rubik's cube building! But why are they being thrown into these different worlds? Read it and find out!
